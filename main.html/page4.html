Click on which option you'd like to explore:

  • The key thing is to make time to practice often speaking, reading, listening and writing in your chosen language where possible.
  • If you have a patient friend or family member who can help with this- use them! If they are distant, video call them and intentionally imbed the language into your conversation.
  • If its a dialect of a language - listen to the local radio if possible.
  • When you can, it is helpful spending time in a place that speaks the language.
  • Language tutor: Verbling has teachers in over 70 languages!
  • Language pen pals: Interpals Language exchange is a great way to meet people and practice learning the language.
  • Language apps: Duolingo and Lingodeer are very interactive and accessible apps! If you are interested in gaining more depth through learning the grammar rules we recomend lingodeer
